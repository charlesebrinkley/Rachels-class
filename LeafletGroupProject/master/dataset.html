Dataset

This data is sourced from the The National Missing and Unidentified Persons System (https://www.namus.gov/). This dataset contains over 10,000 records and includes the following information: Full Name, Age, Race/Ethnicity, and County, City, and State where the missing person was last seen and the Date of Last Contact.
