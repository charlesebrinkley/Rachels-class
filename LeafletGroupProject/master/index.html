Purpose

This website displays missing persons data in the United States. We've created multiple data visualizations breaking down this data. We've created a bar graph that shows the differences of race and ethnicity among missing persons. We've also created a line chart that shows the number of missing persons to show change over time. Finally, we've created two diffrent maps for additional information - a heat map and a map with markers. Explore our site to find more information about missing persons in the U.S.
